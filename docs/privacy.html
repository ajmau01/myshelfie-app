Privacy Policy

At myShelfie, we believe your personal library and reading choices represent an intimate space. We are committed to transparency, security, and giving you absolute control over your digital footprint.

[1] Data We Collect

To deliver a seamless, high-quality reading log and recommendation experience, we collect the following information:

  • Firebase Profile Information: Basic identity details including your User ID (UID), registered email address, display name, and profile photo URL.
  • Library & Social Data: Records of the books you catalog, your shelves and reading states (read, currently reading, to-read, did-not-finish), the formats and sources you read them in, library loans, your taste profile, and your Circle connections, invites, and shared picks.
  • Gemini Prompts: Text prompts and catalog content submitted when generating recommendations, reading tags, or structured book reviews.
  • Barcode/ISBN Queries: ISBN codes queried via barcode scanning or manual searches using the Google Books API to pull cover images and volume details.

[2] How We Use Your Data

Your data is processed strictly to power the core app experience. We ensure that:

  • We will NEVER sell or monetize your data, library habits, or identity details to any third party.
  • We run zero advertisement networks, ad trackers, or advertising SDKs within the platform.
  • Your data powers core features like multi-device syncing, barcode scanning, your reading Portrait, private book recommendations, and your trusted reading Circle.
  • We maintain basic system logs and telemetry purely to verify app stability, debug errors, and monitor API rate limits.

[3] Third-Party Service Providers

We rely on trusted, industry-standard partners to host and secure our infrastructure:

  • Google / Firebase: Powers user authentication, Firestore cloud databases, Cloud Storage for covers, App Hosting, and core API backend execution.
  • Sentry: Captures crash logs and diagnostic traces so we can quickly locate and repair bugs on both the web dashboard and mobile app.

[4] Account Deletion & Data Purge

We honor your absolute right to be forgotten. A self-service Delete Account option is directly accessible within your mobile profile settings and web dashboard.

Tapping this will permanently, instantly, and irreversibly purge all personal library collections, shelves, logs, active sessions, and credential profiles from Firestore, storage buckets, and Firebase Authentication.
