Who it's for

The multi-format reader who's never found their app.

You read a hardcover on the nightstand, borrow three things on Libby, and finish an audiobook on the drive home. No tracker has ever captured that — they make you pretend you read one way. myShelfie is built for the reader who lives across formats and sources, and wants a record that tells the truth.

Built in three layers

The Record, the Portrait, and a small trusted Circle.

Layer 01

The Record

Capture every book, every format, every source — and how it found you: a friend's rec, a book club, browsing, the app's own pick. Multiple formats per book. Library loans with due dates. DNF with a reason, because not finishing is part of the story too.

Layer 02

The Portrait

Your reading life, reflected back. A taste profile that's a mirror, not a search filter — here's what your reading reveals about you. A year-by-year narrative, an "on this day" memory, the shape of how you read. And the better it knows you, the better it points you toward what to read next.

Layer 03

The Circle

A small, trusted graph — just the people you actually read alongside. No follower counts, no public reviews. Honest portraits between people who trust each other become the best book discovery there is — recommendations that come from real reading lives, not an algorithm's guess at them.

What makes it different

First-class for the things other apps flatten.

Format & source
How you read it and where it came from — a pair on every book. Listened to the first half, read the second. Own it and borrowed it.
Provenance
How a book found you is part of the portrait — friend rec, book club, browsing, an in-app pick.
Library loans
Due dates, loan source, a gentle return prompt — not just a "library" tag.
DNF, honestly
"Did not finish," with a reason. It feeds the portrait instead of disappearing.
Visual-first
Cover art facing out, like a bookstore display — your shelf, the way you'd want to see it.
The payoff

Discovery that actually gets you.

Most apps recommend from what you clicked. myShelfie recommends from the truth of how you read — every format, every finish and un-finish, where each book came from, and the readers you trust. The more honestly you log, the more it surprises you in the right way. It grows with you — a record at first, a mirror before long, and, once it truly knows you, a guide to what's next.
